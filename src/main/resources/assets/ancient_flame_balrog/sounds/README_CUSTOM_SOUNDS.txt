Custom Balrog sounds are wired in code and included as starter OGG files:

balrog_roar.ogg        - roar / rage roar
balrog_wings.ogg       - wing beat pulse
balrog_fire.ogg        - fire aura and ranged fire attack
balrog_boss_intro.ogg  - plays once when a player first sees the boss
balrog_slam.ogg        - ground slam impact

You can replace any OGG with your own sound later. Keep the exact same filename.
